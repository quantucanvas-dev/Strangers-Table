What arrives each month

Three things in one envelope.

Sealed, stamped, and waiting in your letterbox.

01

The letter

Letter from a home cook in a specific country and season. Their life, their week, their kitchen. Printed on cream textured paper in a warm serif. Reads in five minutes. Stays with you longer.

02

The recipe card

A postcard-sized illustrated card with the dish from the letter — full ingredients and method. Designed to be pinned, propped on a shelf, or slipped into a recipe box. Something you'll actually use.

03

The ingredient

A small sachet of one key ingredient from that region — saffron, a spice blend, a dried herb. Because some ingredients can't be substituted.
